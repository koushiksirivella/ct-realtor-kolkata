[ Our story ]

Two decades on the
ground in Salt Lake.

Founded in 2004 by Chandra Sekhar Chowdhury, CT Realtor grew from a local Salt Lake desk into one of the area's most trusted estate agencies — serving both corporate names and individual families.

From furnished offices in Sector-V to flats in New Town and HIDCO plots in Rajarhat, the work has always run on the same principle: prioritise people over property. Corporate clients like DLF, DHL, QSpiders, Cosmos Maya and Delta Plus, and hundreds of resident families, have trusted that approach.

"Our vision is acquiring new customers and retaining existing customers by providing top-notch real-estate consultancy in Kolkata — to corporate and individual clients alike."
